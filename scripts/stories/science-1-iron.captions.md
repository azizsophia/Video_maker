## YouTube description
The Quran says, "and We sent down iron, wherein is great might and benefits for the people" (57:25). For centuries, the word "sent down" was a mystery. Then modern science discovered that iron cannot be created on Earth at all. It is forged only in the hearts of massive dying stars, scattered across the universe by supernovae. The iron that became part of Earth sank to form the molten core, whose magnetic field shields all life from the sun's radiation. Even the iron in your blood was made in the stars.
