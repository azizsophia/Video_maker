The Quran says, "He who created seven heavens in layers" (67:3). For most of history, science believed the atmosphere was a single blanket of air. Then, beginning in 1902, scientists sending balloons and rockets ever higher discovered the sky is built in distinct layers, each with its own properties, each shielding the Earth from a different danger above. Seven heavens, in layers, exactly as described 1400 years ago.
