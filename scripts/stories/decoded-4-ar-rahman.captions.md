In this Decoded breakdown we walk its seven movements: the gift of speech, the sun and moon by precise calculation, the Balance of justice, the creation of man and jinn, the two seas that meet but never mix, the truth that all things perish but the Face of your Lord, and the two gardens of Paradise.
