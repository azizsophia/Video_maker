## YouTube description (no emojis)
For a thousand years, Constantinople was the most fortified city on Earth, repelling siege after siege. The Prophet Muhammad foretold it would be conquered (Musnad Ahmad), praising the future commander and his army. He died in 632. The city finally fell on 29 May 1453, to the 21-year-old Sultan Mehmed II, who used massive cannon and hauled 70 ships overland into the Golden Horn, 821 years after the prophecy.

Sources:
- Musnad Ahmad 18957 (the hadith praises the future commander; it does not name him)
- Constantinople fell to Mehmed II on 29 May 1453; the Prophet died in 632
- Mehmed II used large bombards and transported ships overland into the Golden Horn
- Mehmed II is traditionally identified as the praised commander
