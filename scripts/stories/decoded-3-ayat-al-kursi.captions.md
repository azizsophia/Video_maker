Ayat al-Kursi — the Throne Verse, Quran 2:255 — is the verse the Prophet (peace be upon him) named the greatest in the Book of Allah. In this Decoded breakdown we walk through its seven movements, from "there is no god but Him, the Ever-Living" to "His Throne extends over the heavens and the earth," and why it is recited every night for protection and after every prayer.

Sources: the verse is Quran 2:255 (Surah al-Baqarah). The night-protection narration is Sahih al-Bukhari 2311; the "greatest verse" account is Sahih Muslim 810; recitation after every obligatory prayer is Sunan an-Nasa'i (al-Kubra), graded sahih by al-Albani.
